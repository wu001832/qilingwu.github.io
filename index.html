Qiling Wu

PhD Student in Mass Communication, University of Minnesota

I study health communication, misinformation, and correction. My work focuses on how people respond to corrective messages, how communication strategies shape belief updating and sharing intentions, and how digital platforms influence the visibility and effectiveness of correction.

I am particularly interested in theory-based message design, social norms, and AI-assisted interventions for misinformation.
